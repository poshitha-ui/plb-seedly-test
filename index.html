Frequently Asked Questions

What is New Launch Convention 2024 all about?

Join PropertyLimBrothers towards the future of real estate comparisons as we integrate tech

into in-depth analyses of more than 80+ New Launch condominiums in Singapore! There’s no other place like this!

Where will New Launch Convention 2024 be held?

The event will take place at Suntec Convention Centre Hall 404 on the 7th of SEPTEMBER, 2024, from 10:00 am to 8:00 pm.

Will refreshments be provided at New Launch Convention 2024?

Kindly note that refreshments will not be provided during the event.

I would like to bring my children to the event. Do I need to purchase an entry ticket for them?

Entry is free for children under 12 years of age! For smoother registration, we kindly advise bringing your children’s identification, which may be required for age verification purposes.

How can I purchase tickets to New Launch Convention 2024?

Tickets can be purchased here. Otherwise, tickets will also be available for purchase on-site.

What if I want to buy more than two tickets?

You can choose from the buddy ticket (2 pax) or a group ticket (4 pax) options on the ticket purchase section.

How would I know if I have received my ticket?

  1. A confirmation email will be sent to the specific email address entered during the registration process.
  2. You will also receive a unique QR Code which will be your entry method to the New Launch Convention 2024.
  3. If you have purchased any Buddy and Group Tickets, the confirmation email will be sent to Attendee 1’s registered email address.

Am I required to download and print my tickets before attending New Launch Convention 2024?

Simply prepare your confirmation ticket (both hard or soft copies allowed) on the event day and present the QR code upon arrival at the registration counter.

I’ve made payment for my tickets, but I didn’t get the confirmation email. What should I do?

If you do not receive a confirmation email within the next couple of minutes after your purchase, please check your spam folder. Kindly ensure that you have provided the correct email address. If the issue persists, please contact our PLBCare Team at: +65 9745 7388.

Is there a refund policy?

We kindly inform you that our policy does not permit refunds. We apologise for any inconvenience.

Can I share my ticket with a friend?

Tickets are mandatory for attendees above the age of 12 and are associated with the purchaser’s name; therefore, they are non-transferable. We appreciate your understanding.

How do I make payment for the entry tickets?

  1. Payments will be securely processed through Stripe, where you can choose to pay via Credit or Debit Card.
  2. On-site payments for walk-ins are also available on the event day via Paynow.
